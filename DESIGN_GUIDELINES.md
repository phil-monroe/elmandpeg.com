# Elm and Peg - Brand Design Guidelines

## Overview
These design guidelines define the visual identity for **Elm and Peg**, a Customer Success consulting firm. The brand aesthetics reflect growth, partnership, established roots, and natural flourishing through an earthy, grounded, and professional style.

---

## 1. Logo & Imagery
* **Concept:** Two intertwined trees growing and flourishing together, complete with deep roots to symbolize a strong foundation and partnership.
* **Style:** Clean, professional line-art.
* **Colors:** * Trunk and roots are drawn in **Trunk Brown**.
  * Leaves feature a variation of two distinct green tones (**Forest Green** and **Sage Green**). No brown leaves are used.
* **Format:** Tight, square crop ensuring no dead space, optimized for easy website embedding and digital use. No text is included in the standalone logo mark.

---

## 2. Color Palette
Our color palette is directly inspired by the natural, earthy tones of the logo, providing a warm, clean, and professional feel.

| Color Name | Hex Code | Primary Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Trunk Brown** | `#4A3E38` | Main headers (H1, H2), large text elements, and primary navigational links. |
| **Forest Green** | `#3E5E43` | Primary call-to-action (CTA) buttons (e.g., "Contact Us", "Learn More") and highlighted text/keywords. |
| **Sage Green** | `#597E5D` | Feature section backgrounds, custom illustrations, and subtle text accents. |
| **Paper Cream** | `#FAF8F2` | Main landing page background. Provides a warm, professional canvas. |
| **Warm Gray** | `#EBE8E1` | Secondary background panels, subtle UI dividers, and card borders. |
| **Off-Black** | `#2B2B2B` | Main body copy text. Ensures maximum readability while remaining softer than a pure black. |

---

## 3. Typography
Our typography utilizes free, highly accessible Google Fonts that balance a modern UI feel with the organic, established nature of the Elm and Peg brand.

### Brand & Headings: **Montserrat**
* **Style:** Modern, geometric sans-serif. Clean and professional.
* **Usage:** Company name ("ELM AND PEG"), primary headers, and major section titles.
* **Weight/Casing:** Use bold weights and all-caps for the primary brand name to replicate the established, grounded feel of the logo.

### Body Text & UI Elements: **Open Sans**
* **Style:** Highly readable, neutral, utility sans-serif.
* **Usage:** Main body paragraphs, success stories, consulting framework details, forms, and general UI text.
* **Weight/Casing:** Regular/400 weight for general reading. Strong legibility across all device sizes.

---

## 4. UI/UX Application Summary
When designing the Elm and Peg landing page or presentation materials:
1. Set the main background to **Paper Cream**.
2. Use **Off-Black** (`Open Sans`) for long-form reading text to reduce eye strain.
3. Structure the page hierarchy using **Trunk Brown** (`Montserrat`) for major section headers.
4. Draw the user's eye to conversion points using **Forest Green** buttons.
5. Break up long sections of content using alternating backgrounds of **Paper Cream** and **Warm Gray**, or use **Sage Green** for specialized callout banners.
